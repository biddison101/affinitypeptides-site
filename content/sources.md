title: "Scientific Sources"
---

All research on Affinity Peptides is based on primary scientific literature.

Major sources include:

- PubMed  
- National Institutes of Health (NIH)  
- New England Journal of Medicine  
- Nature  
- Cell  
- Science  
- FDA clinical trial databases  

Individual research articles are cited directly on each peptide and study page.

This platform prioritizes:
- Peer-reviewed evidence  
- Human clinical trials when available  
- Mechanistic biological research  

No information is taken from blogs, forums, or non-scientific media.
