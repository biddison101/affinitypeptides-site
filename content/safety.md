---

Affinity Peptides exists as a **scientific information platform** only.

We do not:
- Provide medical advice  
- Diagnose or treat any condition  
- Prescribe medications  
- Sell pharmaceutical products  

All information presented on this website is intended strictly for **educational and research purposes**.

Peptides discussed on this site are:
- Investigational compounds  
- Research chemicals  
- Studied in laboratory and clinical research settings  

Any human use of peptides must be supervised by a licensed medical professional.

Readers are responsible for verifying information and consulting qualified healthcare providers before making health decisions.
